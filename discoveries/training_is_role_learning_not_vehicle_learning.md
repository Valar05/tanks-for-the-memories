# Discovery: Training is role learning, not vehicle learning

## Discovery
A young tank commander is being trained to perform a role: observe, report, coordinate, and control. The tank is the medium, not the lesson.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/31_jacob-l-devers.md:15-18 - combined-arms thinking and a German tank platoon manual translation frame the doctrine.
- source_library/32_fm-17-12-tank-gunnery.md:15-17 - gunnery drills are only one layer of training.
- source_library/33_fm-17-30-tank-platoon.md:15-17 - platoon organization, movement, and command responsibilities are core.
- source_library/34_fm-17-33-the-tank-battalion-light-and-medium.md:15-17 - battalion doctrine defines the role set beyond tank-vs-tank dueling.

## Why It Matters
This is the clearest reason the game should train command habits instead of only vehicle handling.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `commander_training_level`
- Definition: How well the commander has learned platoon leadership, gunnery, and combined arms.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `trust_in_commander`, `report_accuracy`

## Mechanic Candidate
Role-based training loop with drills for observation, reporting, and coordination.

## Pressure System Impact
Better training reduces errors in every other pressure system.
