# Discovery: Occupation is a second game mode

## Discovery
Once territory is controlled, the simulation should shift into administration, patrol, curfew, requisition, and civilian management.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/44_military_government.md:15-17 - military government covers temporary authority, curfews, checkpoints, requisition, and command responsibility.
- source_library/45_military_occupation.md:15-17 - occupation defines how control is established and how authority behaves in occupied space.
- source_library/51_office_of_military_government_united_states.md:15-17 - occupation brings denazification and administrative burden.
- source_library/54_military_police_corps_united_states.md:15-17 - military police keep routes, prisoners, and rear areas functioning.

## Why It Matters
Victory should open a different loop, not terminate the simulation.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `occupation_load`
- Definition: Administrative and security burden created by controlled territory.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `civilian_contact`, `administrative_backlog`

## Mechanic Candidate
Post-combat governance layer with patrols, paperwork, and legitimacy management.

## Pressure System Impact
Captured ground keeps demanding attention and manpower.
