# Discovery: Capture is a branch, not an end

## Discovery
POW life needs its own state model because camp type, labor assignment, winter exposure, and legal status all change the lived experience after capture.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/59_prisoner_of_war_camp.md:15-17 - detention, confinement, guard duty, and memory all matter after surrender.
- source_library/60_german_prisoner_of_war_camps_world_war_ii.md:15-17 - camp type, labor, and nationality alter treatment.
- source_library/64_stalag_ii_d.md:15-17 - a concrete camp example includes winter, nourishment, and civilian/prisoner conditions.

## Why It Matters
A surrender or capture event should branch the story instead of terminating it.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `captivity_stress`
- Definition: Stress caused by confinement, uncertainty, and deprivation after capture.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `camp_type`, `loss_memory`

## Mechanic Candidate
Captivity mode with camp types, labor, and release paths.

## Pressure System Impact
Capture creates a separate life state that still generates decisions and memory.
