# Discovery: Fatigue degrades orders before firepower

## Discovery
Under prolonged stress, the first thing to fail is not the gun; it is judgment, sequencing, and the ability to keep a coherent plan in mind.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/40_combat-stress-reaction.md:15-17 - fatigue becomes operationally visible through stress reaction and unit cohesion loss.
- source_library/41_united_states_army_air_forces.md:15-17 - the Army used rotation policy because exhaustion had real effectiveness costs.
- source_library/50_army_ground_forces.md:15-17 - replacements and training are the institutional answer to degraded effectiveness.

## Why It Matters
This suggests a command-degradation system where options, sequencing, and recall get worse before raw combat ability fully collapses.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `fatigue`
- Definition: Accumulated physical and cognitive exhaustion.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `decision_quality`, `reaction_time`

## Mechanic Candidate
Progressive command degradation, not just stat penalties.

## Pressure System Impact
Tired crews produce worse plans, slower reaction, and more mistakes than healthy crews.
