# Discovery: Mail is morale infrastructure

## Discovery
Letters and packages should be treated like an infrastructure system because they control home attachment, censorship, and the emotional rhythm of long campaigns.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/46_military_mail.md:15-17 - military mail explicitly shapes morale and memory.
- source_library/43_united_states_army_during_world_war_ii.md:15-17 - daily life includes repetitive food, hygiene, and boredom, which mail interrupts.
- source_library/55_chaplain_corps_united_states_army.md:15-17 - moral support and grief processing sit adjacent to mail in unit life.

## Why It Matters
Mail arrival can be a meaningful event, not a cosmetic flavor line.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `mail_latency`
- Definition: Time between sending and receiving home contact.
- Range: 0-30
- Related: `home_attachment`, `morale_bonus_from_mail`
