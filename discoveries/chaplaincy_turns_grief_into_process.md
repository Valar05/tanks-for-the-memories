# Discovery: Chaplaincy turns grief into process

## Discovery
Loss needs ritual, counseling, and memorialization; otherwise grief stays unprocessed and leaks into cohesion and command burden.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/55_chaplain_corps_united_states_army.md:15-17 - chaplains provide moral support, grief response, and funerals.
- source_library/19_bomb-tank.md:15-17 - replacement personnel turn the tank into a memory vessel.
- source_library/40_combat-stress-reaction.md:15-17 - stress reaction affects unit cohesion.

## Why It Matters
The simulation should store named losses and social consequences, not just casualty counts.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `grief_load`
- Definition: Accumulated emotional burden from losses and funerals.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `moral_support`, `loss_memory`

## Mechanic Candidate
Memorial, counseling, and grief-recovery loop.

## Pressure System Impact
Unprocessed loss makes later decisions worse.
