# Discovery: Route control and security are supply gameplay

## Discovery
Road control, checkpoints, prisoner movement, and convoy security are core logistics problems, not background patrol tasks.

## Historical Evidence
- source_library/54_military_police_corps_united_states.md:15-17 - military police handle traffic control, POWs, route security, and rear-area order.
- source_library/61_feldgendarmerie.md:15-17 - Feldgendarmerie handles road traffic control and checkpoint security.
- source_library/59_prisoner_of_war_camp.md:15-17 - detention and guard duty consume resources.

## Why It Matters
If the road is blocked, the tank unit is not just late; it is starved, stalled, or forced to reroute.

## Simulation Variable
- Name: `route_control`
- Definition: How effectively roads, convoys, and checkpoints are managed.
- Range: 0-100
- Related: `traffic_delay`, `detention_load`

## Mechanic Candidate
Convoy, checkpoint, and prisoner-escort management.

## Pressure System Impact
Security friction directly slows supply, evacuation, and tempo.
